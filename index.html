How to play
  • Tap tiles to uncover the hidden safe path from S to the door (D).
  • Find the key (K) first — the door unlocks only after you collect it.
  • A wrong tile resets the board (Hard mode hides all previous progress; Easy mode keeps revealed tiles).
  • Reach D to clear the level.
  • Restart to replay, or use Next Level to skip ahead (only completed levels count toward finishing all five).
